A fork of SideScreen

Telemachus began with SideScreen by Tran Vuong Quoc Dat and its contributors. Their open-source work established the virtual display, capture, video transport, Android receiver, wireless pairing, and touch foundation.

Thank you to every SideScreen maintainer and contributor. View the original project

Telemachus adds existing-display mirroring, cable-driven reconnection, latency-focused pacing and backpressure, capture recovery, new onboarding, security hardening, and its own Companion Screens identity.
